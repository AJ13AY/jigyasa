CA FOUNDATION

CA Foundation is an entry-level test designed for students who want to pursue the CA Course. Exams for the ICAI CA Foundation Course are conducted twice a year in June and December. CA INTERMEDIATE

A Intermediate is the second level exam, of a course in India, Chartered Accountancy. It has eight subjects and over 7000 pages of study material that a student is expected to cover in the nine months study period allotted to them.

CA FINAL

CA final is the last level course to be completed for an aspirant of Chartered Accountancy. Once the student has cleared the Intermediate level, he/she enters the last leg of CA, i.e., CA Final Course. ... CA final exam can be attempted in the last 6 months of articleship training, including excess leaves, if any.
